HOW TO VOTE

Step1

  • click the Registration on home page.
  • Fill your information in registration form.
  • then click on submit button.
  • Then you can see the message on your screen that "YOUR DATA SUCCESSFULLY REGISTERED.".
  • Go back to home .

Step2

  • click the Login on home page.
  • Fill your information in that required for login form.
  • then click on Login button.
  • You can see the New page open in your screen

Step3

  • Select any of one party which you want to see as a Winner.
  • then click on Submit button.
  • Then you can see the message on your screen that "YOU HAVE SUCCESSFULLY VOTED".
